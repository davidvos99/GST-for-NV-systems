The files in this folder contain the circuit list experiments created by the pygsti.circuits.create_lsgst_circuits method.
Germs and fiducials were generated with: fidsel.find_fiducials(target_model) and germsel.find_germs(target_model)
The different circuit lists are:

3 gate (I, 0.5pi X, 0.5pi Y) with generated fiducials and germs 
3 gate (I, 0.5pi X, 0.5pi Y) with fiducials and germs from paper
5 gate (I, 0.5pi X, 0.5pi Y, pi X, pi Y) with generated fiducials and germs
